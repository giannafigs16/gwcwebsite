Corgi's

Hypoallergenic: No

Life expectancy: 12 to 15 years

Temperament: Tenacious, Bold, Outgoing, Playful, Friendly, Protective

Colors: Fawn, Black & Tan, Sable, Black & White, Blue, Red

Chow Chow's

Hypoallergenic: No

Life expectancy: 9 to 15 years

Temperament: Aloof, Loyal, Independent, Quiet

Colors: Black, Blue, Fawn, Cream, Red
